💻 WhyForecast

Welcome to WhyForecast! This is an online research study about a type of machine learning known as time series forecasting. The study consists of a short tutorial introducing the topic, followed by a series of multiple-choice exercises based on some real-world examples, and concludes with a short questionnaire. It will take roughly 25 minutes to complete.😃


Ethics approval for this experiment was granted on 04/04/2024 by the University of Bristol Faculty of Engineering Research Ethics Committee, with the reference code 17907. If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback, please feel free to contact Hongnan Ma at the University of Bristol.
